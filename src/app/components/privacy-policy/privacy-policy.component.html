Cookies

We use "cookies" to store certain information about you and track your website visits. Cookies are commonly used on websites to enhance user experience. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your web browser and stored on your computer's hard drive. Unless you delete or disable cookies, our servers will be notified of your visits and patterns of usage each time you access our website using the same computer. We use cookies to identify you and allow you to access your account and registration information without having to re-enter it, gather statistical information about user behavior, analyze browsing patterns, help target advertisements based on user interests, assist our partners in monitoring user visits and processing orders, and track participation and progress in promotions.

You can choose whether and how cookies are accepted by configuring your web browser. You can accept all cookies, be notified when a cookie is sent, or decline all cookies. If you choose to decline cookies, you may need to re-enter information more frequently and some features of our website may be unavailable.
